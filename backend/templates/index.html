About Us

Course Reviews is a not-for-profit startup dedicated to bringing the best reviews on both online courses and offline lectures.

Course Review believes that everyone has the right to view whether a course they are taking will be worth its value or not. In this website, we aim to achieve the following:

  • Inform students about the value of a course they are planning to take.
  • Promote a more transparent education system.
  • Push the boundaries of the education's quality through honest criticism.
